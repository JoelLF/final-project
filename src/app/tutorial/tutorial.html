Hunter Visit

Discover through experiencing Gran Canaria

What is Hunter Visit?

Hunter Visit is an application that makes you explore the whole Gran Canaria in a fun way with a high quality mobile app!

How to play?

Hunter Visit is a set of questions and marker points on top of a map, answer the questions correctly to unlock new markers and info about them! If you want to view the info about a specific marker agin, tap on the correct answer.
