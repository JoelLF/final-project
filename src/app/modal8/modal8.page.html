Palace Rodríguez Quegles

The Rodríguez Quegles Palace was built in 1900 by Don Juan Rodríguez Quegles. This building is used for different events like cinema, music ...

Don Domingo Rodríguez Quegles, promised his wife that he was going to build the "prettiest and most sumptuous" house in Las Palmas for live near to his mother, and built this sober eclectic building.

About decoration Quegles brought the most exclusive materials in the world for the construction of this house as traveled to France where they brought the decorative lions and French workshops windows with floral and country logos, they also used elements of African and Cuban noble woods and it has Masonic elements in glassware and Egyptian inspired columns on the facade.
